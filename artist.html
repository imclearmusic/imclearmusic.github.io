About him


iMCleAr_ is the name of a French musician who started his musical adventure in June 2024. Well in fact, his passion for music existed years before he stared composing/producing music. He remembers listening to his parent's CD's in the car, not understanding a word of the lyrics, but still enjoying the experience. Music have always been the most important thing in his life, and being able to create songs was a huge achievement, something he always dreamed of! Since the moment he got his hand on his beloved Minilab 3, the keyboard that started it all, iMCleAr_ never stopped creating, exploring hundreds of differents musical genres, as a tribute to the diversity of music. iMCleAr_ has only gotten better, both in terms of equipment and experience, which allows him to produce something new with each composition released, and which makes him a special artist, without a predefined genre, but with a very particular musical touch.
So if you wanna discover his universe, go stream his songs on Spotify!
